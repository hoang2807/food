Microsoft is the largest company in the computer industry. Specialized in the design of operating systems and office software for individuals and companies of all sizes, Microsoft offers a set of software solutions adapted to all needs.

Founded in 1975, Microsoft quickly established itself as a key player in the IT world with the release of DOS, and concretized its leading position with the successive releases of Microsoft Windows in 1985, and the Microsoft Office office suite in 1989.

The adaptation and constant development of Microsoft software to the needs of each customer has led to the implementation of a licensing system to meet all requests:

  • OPEN (MOLP) licenses - Available in electronic version through the dedicated portal VLSC (Volume Licensing Service Center)
  • OEM licenses - dedicated and assigned to specific physical equipment (server, laptop, desktop)
  • FPP (BOX) licenses - standard boxed version, most often chosen by individuals.
